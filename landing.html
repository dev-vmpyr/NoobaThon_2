Amenities

Time Square [T.S.]

Time Square is the arguably the most memorable place in the whole college. Claimed to be the place where most of the memories are made. Chilling with friends, celebrating birthdays and much more, T.S. is the place where you go.

Central Library

Acclaimed to be having all the books with both analog and digital libraries, the Central Library of NITW has got every bit of information you will ever need. Be it for revising lectures or doing research, the Central Library's got your back.

Ultra Mega Hostel [1.8k]

The Aisa's 2nd Largest Hostel and the Largest in the whole South India, Ultra Mega Hostel of more popularly known as 1.8k is the place where students will have the best of their hostel experience. Well maintained, clean and hygenic, its the best hostel.

Stadium

The NITW stadium is the place where atheletes enjoy coming to everyday. It is a motivation to new sportsmen to give their best to the sport. The stadium has everything from the running tracks to playgrounds for cricket, football, vollyball,basketball and much more with state of the art facilities.

Food Street

The food street is the place where all the students go to eat snacks with friends and chill for a while. It is full of different canteen's, ice-cream points, juice stalls, and fast foods both vegeterian and non-vegeterian.
